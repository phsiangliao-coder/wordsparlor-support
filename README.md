## Privacy Policy for Words Parlor

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

1. **Introduction:** Welcome to Words Parlor. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains how we handle your information when you play our game.

2. **Information We Collect:** We generally do not collect "Personally Identifiable Information" (PII) such as your real name, physical address, or phone number. However, to operate the game and provide services, we (or our third-party providers) may collect:

* Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID).

* Usage Data: Details on how you use the game (e.g., levels completed, time spent, button clicks) to help us improve the experience.

* Location Data: General location data based on IP address (country or city level) for analytics and ad personalization.

3. **Third-Party Services:** We use third-party services to support our game (such as for analytics, cloud saves, or advertising). These partners have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

* Analytics: We use Firebase / Google Analytics to understand game usage.

Note: You can opt out of personalized tracking in your device settings.
